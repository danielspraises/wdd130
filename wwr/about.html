PrimeWave Luxury Rafting

PrimeWave Luxury Rafting exists to redefine adventure in Lagos by merging the raw power of nature with the elegance of premium comfort. Our purpose is to create extraordinary water-based experiences that inspire exploration and celebrate the natural beauty of Nigeria’s landscapes. Guided by our mission to deliver safe, world-class rafting adventures, we operate with a creed rooted in integrity, respect for the environment, and exceptional guest care. At PrimeWave, we believe that adventure should be thrilling, rejuvenating, and luxurious. Our vision is to be the leading rafting company in Nigeria, known for our innovative approach to adventure tourism and our commitment to excellence.

History

Founded in Lagos, Nigeria, Lagoon Luxe Rafting began with a passion for blending thrilling adventure with premium comfort. What started as a simple idea—bringing world-class rafting experiences to Nigeria’s coastal and river landscapes—quickly grew into a full-fledged adventure brand known for safety, elegance, and unforgettable moments. Inspired by Lagos’ vibrant waterways and the untamed beauty of Nigeria’s natural environment, the company was built to provide an escape from the ordinary. Over the years, Lagoon Luxe Rafting has evolved into a pioneer of luxury rafting tourism, offering exclusive guided expeditions, curated nature journeys, and refined outdoor experiences that allow guests to connect with nature without sacrificing comfort.
